# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Bartenders and bar managers in professional cocktail bars. They use the app during service (behind the bar, on a phone) and during prep (batch production, ordering). Primary context: low light, fast pace, often with wet/busy hands. Secondary context: planning sessions at a desk.

## Product Purpose

Mixtura centralises everything a cocktail bar needs: recipe management, stock tracking, batch production tools, and team communication. Success looks like a bartender opening the app mid-service and finding what they need in two taps, with zero friction.

## Brand Personality

Premium, precise, nocturnal. The feel of a well-lit back bar at 10 pm — polished glass, dark surfaces, warm amber light. Not flashy, not loud. Confident and expert.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards (cluttered metric grids, blue primary buttons)
- Bright white "restaurant app" aesthetics (Toast POS, Square)
- Over-animated apps that feel slow under pressure

## Design Principles

1. **Dark is the default** — the app lives in dim environments; the dark theme is the canonical experience, light mode is a courtesy.
2. **Gold earns its place** — the amber accent signals action and importance; don't dilute it by applying it to decorative elements.
3. **Density without clutter** — bartenders are time-pressured; information density matters, but never at the cost of legibility.
4. **Tactile depth** — surfaces should feel like physical materials (dark glass, matte metal), not flat rectangles.
5. **Motion serves speed** — transitions confirm state changes; they should be fast and purposeful, never decorative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum for text contrast on both dark and light themes
- Touch targets ≥ 44px (mobile-first, often used with one hand)
- Reduced motion respected (fast crossfades as alternative)
